Goals

Badlav Sansthan always believes in “God helps those who helps themselves” and thus replicates the same. It acts as a “Catalyst” to motivate the people and ensure people participation by involving in the process of planning, implementing and monitoring the program.

Main Objectives

1) The Institute will remove the existing Orthodox Thoughts and establish a Progressive Scientific Ideology (PSI) in the society for making the people self- reliant in this Scientific Era.
2) Badlav Sansthan will promote the Educational Institutions to eradicate the illiteracy and implement the pogrammes particularly related to the Women Education and Literacy Campaigns. Institute will also strengthen the Vocational Technical Education (VTE).

3) Institute will provide Health Care Services (HCS) through implementing the Hospitals.
4) Institute will formulate Rural Development Schemes to boost up the better economy status of Dalits, Tribes and Other Backward caste people and implement its through co-operation of local people. It will also provide the job opportunities to the self reliant citizen of this section. .
5) Institute will disseminate the Innovative Technology Information (ITI) to the farmers related to changes occurring in Agricultural sector and will establish the Kisan Ekta Kendras for this cause.
6) Institute will organize the cultural, sports and competitive activities and establish the Folk Art Centers, and Exercise Centers for this cause.
7) Establishment of Environment Protection Centers to protect the Environment.
8) Institute will work for Human Rights and it will implement the programmes those are against all type of Human Based Exploitation and Injustice and will execute the campaigns to eradicate the drug Addiction and alcoholism.
9) Provide Emergency Services in such conditions e.g. Drought, Earth Quakes, Fire, Accidents, Communal Riots, and Epidemic etc.
10) Institute will implement the welfare programmes for Children, Students, Aged, Tortured, Physically Handicapped, Orphans and Deserted Women.
11) Institute will organize the collective marriages in the direction of end the existing discrimination and bringing out the all round changes in the Society.

About Working Area

Rajasthan State is a geographically and constitutionally working area of this organization. It is working in Tribal Areas of Southern Rajasthan on various issues. This area is characterized by undulating upland with high slopes covered out with separate barren hillock and valley where villages are scattered in small hamlets. The region is predominated by Tribal population having agriculture as a main source of livelihood, but due to unfertile, undulating land, small land holding makes the farming only of subsistence type. Drought is regular and general natural calamity in the area and thus starvation and migration is a subsequent result. Institution has decided to start to grass root level activities in other parts of state recently.

Centers Coordination Committee (C3)

Badlav Sansthan has formed a centers coordination committee (C3) through clubbing its centers.
It is headed by President of the Institute. It will meet quarterly in whole year to discuss and formulate various rules and regulations.
