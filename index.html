genesis of organization

Badlav Sansthan is an Organization which is devoted for Social Change, All -Round Development and Human Rights of the Society. It is a Non-Political, Non-Governmental and Non -Profit Making Voluntary Organization.

It was established by a group of young Scientists in the Dynamic Leadership of Dr. ShriRam Arya at Udaipur, Rajasthan on September, 19, 1998. It was created on the felt need to have a platform with committed workforce to initiate the process of development at the grass root level with People Organization.

It follows an integrated Rural and Urban Developmental Approach. It is dedicated to the Enlightenment of Rural and Urban Populace with special attention to downtrodden underprivileged, marginalized and weaker sections of the Society.

conceptual background

Change is an ever present phenomenon at everywhere. The Nature is never at rest. It is changeful. Change is an ever present in the world, because change is the law of Nature. Similarly, society is not at all a static phenomenon, but it is a dynamic entity. It is an “on-going process”. Society is subject to constant Changes. Social Change has occurred in all the society itself. Every society and culture, no matter how traditional and conservative, is constantly undergoing change. Society changes in ceaseless flux and flow. Without change any development can not take place at anywhere in the World. People too must change and acquire the facility of change.

Statutory Status

Badlav Sansthan is a Voluntary Organization which is dedicated to the cause of Social Change of Rural and Urban masses since its inception. It is registered under Rajasthan Society Registration Act, 1958 from Registrar office of Udaipur District in Rajasthan. It is also registered under the FCRA, 80G, 12A, PAN etc. from various Governmental bodies.
